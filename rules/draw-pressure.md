# Draw Pressure & Shields

When someone plays a **2** or **Black Jack**, the next player is under **draw pressure**.

---

## What is Draw Pressure?

You're under draw pressure when facing an accumulated draw penalty that you must respond to.

## Important Notes

- You **cannot start a run** while under draw pressure
- You must **stack, shield, or draw first**
- Draw totals **add up**: 2+2+Black Jack = +9 cards
